How To Chat?

To Chat with our College Enquiry Chatbot,
# Click on the button below in the bottom right corner of your screen.
# A Pop-Up Window will appear in the right side of the screen.
# You can type in the message you want to ask to our Chatbot in the Text box below, inside the window.
# Then press Enter and you are good to go with chatting with our College Enquiry Chatbot.
# Our Chatbot will answer all your queries.
# Once Completed Chatting You can close the Chat window by clicking on the cross mark at the above right corner of the window.
