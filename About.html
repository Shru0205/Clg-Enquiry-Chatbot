About

The College Enquiry Chatbot focusses on anwering the queries of students related to the colleges affilated under Mumbai University. College Enquiry Chatbot aims to provide the information about various colleges under Mumbai University, regarding the admission procedures, Fee structure, and various other administrational questions. It would also provide the students with the list of colleges under Mumbai Universty. The College enquiry chatbot can help deal with these problems by just chatting with the bot instead of visiting the website and exploring it. In case if the user needs assistance from the college website itself it would also provide the user with the particular colleges' website link.
